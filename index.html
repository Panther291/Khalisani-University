Best University in Howrah | Academic & Placement Excellence Since 1969
As one of the best universities in Howrah, Khalisani University offers 69 years of academic excellence in curated career-building UG, PG, PhD, Diploma programmes, with 360-degree placement support from a proactive placement team.
