Khalisani University
Best University in Howrah | Academic & Placement Excellence Since 1969
As one of the best universities in Howrah, Khalisani University offers 69 years of academic excellence in curated career-building UG, PG, PhD, Diploma programmes, with 360-degree placement support from a proactive placement team. Extensive laboratory hours, advanced research stations, international collaboration, live projects, industry-institute-interface programmes, varsity-industry and hospital tie ups, top faculties with rich industry experience, intensive pre-placement training on a global learning campus make up for a one-of-a-kind learning experience.
